## Question: Where should the city prioritize resources based on the most frequent complaints by agency, borough, and problem type?
# Answer:
1. Expand Housing Services (HPD)

Heating/hot water complaints dominate several boroughs, especially the Bronx and Brooklyn.
Action: Increase inspectors, winter emergency response teams, landlord enforcement.

2. Increase Parking Enforcement (NYPD / DOT)

Illegal parking is the single largest issue in Brooklyn and Queens.
Action: Add ticketing units, towing, traffic camera monitoring.

3. Address Noise Complaints

Residential noise is high in Bronx, Brooklyn, and Manhattan.
Action: Night patrols, mediation teams, targeted enforcement.

4. Seasonal Public Works Support

Snow/Ice complaints are high in Staten Island and Brooklyn.
Action: Snow removal crews, salt deployment, sidewalk enforcement.

5. Overall

the city should focus first on:

Housing repairs (heat/hot water)
Illegal parking enforcement
Noise response teams
Street maintenance & snow removal

These areas affect the most residents and generate the highest complaint volumes.
